# 10 — Reading & Media

Master list, organized by **format**, tagged by **subdomain** and source type. `[primary]` = the person/org's own work, a peer-reviewed paper, a foundational talk, or a community-maintained resource. `[secondary]` = journalism, analysis, explainer. `[commerce]` = has a sales motive; read accordingly. `[verify]` = confirm citation/link/availability before relying on it. Dates noted where they matter — an old claim is a historical data point, not current state.

Detail on why each matters lives in the subdomain file cited. No affiliate links; no purchase recommendations.
